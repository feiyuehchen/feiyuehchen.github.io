Fei-Yueh Chen 陳飛岳

I am a master student from AIR lab, Department of ECE and Facts.lab, Department of Linguistics in the Univeristy of Rochester. I'm also a member in CCL Lab, Department of Economics, National Taiwan University. I focus on applications of Large Language Models (LLMs), especially in Music and Social Science fields.

Before studying in U of Rochester, I was the research assistant in NLPSA and Music AI Lab, IIS, Academia Sinica, Taiwan. I received a bachlor degree in Economics (major) and Mathmatics (minor) from National Taiwan University.

I play music, take photos and write stories in my free time.
